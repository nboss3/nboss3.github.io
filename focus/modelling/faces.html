Poke

Splits selected faces into a triangle fan with a center vertex.

Also see: Blender Manual

Wireframe

Creates a solid wireframe structure from selected faces.

Also see: Blender Manual

Extrude Individual

Extrudes each selected face separately along local normals.

Also see: Blender Manual

Beautify

Rearranges selected faces to try and get less degenerated geometry.

Also see: Blender Manual

Face Menu

Switches the pie menu to the default blender face menu.

Also see: Blender Manual

Grid Fill

Fills an n-gon face with a grid based on surrounding geometry.

Also see: Blender Manual

Extrude Normal

Extrudes region along local normals.

Also see: Blender Manual

Solidify

Creates a solid skin by extruding, compensating for sharp angles.

Also see: Blender Manual
