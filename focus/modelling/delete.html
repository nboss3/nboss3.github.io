Delete Vertices

Deletes selected vertices.

Also see: Blender Manual

Dissolve Vertices

Removes selected vertices without creating holes.

Also see: Blender Manual

Dissolve Edges

Removes selected edges without creating holes.

Also see: Blender Manual

Dissolve Faces

Removes selected faces without creating holes.

Also see: Blender Manual

Delete Faces

Deletes selected faces.

Also see: Blender Manual

Only Edge & Faces

Deletes only selected edges and faces, vertices are preserved.

Also see: Blender Manual

Only Faces

Deletes selected faces, vertices and edges are preserved.

Also see: Blender Manual

Remove Doubles

Merges vertices within a certain distance of each other.

Also see: Blender Manual

Delete Edges

Deletes selected edges.

Also see: Blender Manual

Edge Collapse

Merges each selected edge into single vertices.

Also see: Blender Manual

Delete Edge Loops

Effectively dissolves the selected edge loop. Creating one loop of faces where there was two.

Also see: Blender Manual

Limited Dissolve

Dissolves vertices and edges on flat regions of the mesh.

Also see: Blender Manual
