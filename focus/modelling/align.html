Align X

Moves selected elements to the same point on the x-axis, effectively flattening vertices on the x-axis.

Align to X-0

Moves selected elements to the zero point on the x-axis.

Align to Y-0

Moves selected elements to the zero point on the y-axis.

Align to Z-0

Moves selected elements to the zero point on the z-axis.

Align Z

Moves selected elements to the same point on the z-axis, effectively flattening vertices on the z-axis.

Align Positive / Negative

Aligns selected elements to the furthest front or back element along the selected axis. The vertex with the largest or smallest coordinate on the chosen axis becomes the origin for the alignment.

Align Y

Moves selected elements to the same point on the y-axis, effectively flattening vertices on the y-axis.
