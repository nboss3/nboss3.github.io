The functions from left to right;

  • Adds a plane mirrored on the x-axis
  • Adds an 8-sided cylinder

  • Adds a cube transformed to sphere with 1 subdivision
  • Adds a cube transformed to sphere with 3 subdivisions
  • Adds a cube transformed to sphere with 7 subdivisions
  • Adds a single vertex
  • Adds a cylinder with an extruded hole through it
