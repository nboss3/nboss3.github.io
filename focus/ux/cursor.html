Cursor to Center

Places the 3D cursor at the viewport origin or point (0,0,0)

Also see: Blender Manual

Cursor to Active

Moves the 3D cursor to the origin of the active object.

Also see: Blender Manual

Selection to Cursor (Offset)

Moves the selected objects to the 3D cursor in a way such that their median point is centred at the 3D cursor.

Also see: Blender Manual

Cursor to Grid

Places the 3D cursor at the nearest grid coordinate.

Also see: Blender Manual

Selected & Cursor to Center

Moves both the 3D cursor and selected objects to the center of the world.

Also see: Blender Manual

Selection to Grid

Moves selected objects median to the nearest grid point.

Also see: Blender Manual

Selection to Cursor

Moves all selected objects to the 3D cursor point.

Also see: Blender Manual

Cursor to Selected

Moves the 3D cursor to the center of the current selected, based on the current pivot point.

Also see: Blender Manual
