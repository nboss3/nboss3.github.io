Bio

I'm currently working as a freelance artist and studying electrical engineering in Brisbane, Australia. I am the developer of the Focus plugin for the open source software, Blender.

Both art and engineering have been a strong part of my life. I have been interested in computers ever since I was 6, and beginning my artistic journey when I was 12. Most of all, I love combining the two, working on things like synthesizers, CG software and analog technology.

Growing up, I learnt many different software packages, exploring digital solutions to problems. Lately however, I have been intrigued by how traditional analog electronics can solve problems in science, engineering and art. Building analog computing modules and synthesizer circuits to create art and solve problems in a very interesting way.
