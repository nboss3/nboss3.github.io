Convex Hull

Takes a point cloud and creates a hull geometry around it.

Also see: Blender Manual

Symmetrize

Makes the mesh being edited symmetrical across chosen axis.

Also see: Blender Manual

Knife Project

Uses objects to cut into a mesh to create geometry. The angle at which it cuts is dependent on the view axis.

Also see: Blender Manual

Mesh Menu

Switches the pie menu to the default face menu.

Also see: Blender Manual

Bisect

Cuts the mesh in two along a selected plane.

Also see: Blender Manual

Snap to Symmetry

A snapping tool that allows you to snap vertices to their mirrored counterpart.

Also see: Blender Manual
