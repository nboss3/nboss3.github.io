Page Setup

17/01/2022 | General

I have decided to document my experiments and work with kumiko and shōji 「建具工芸」 in a blog. I am probably ten years behind the times in doing this, but I can always convert to a more modern video blog or YouTube channel at anytime. I will try to keep content on this blog and content on my Instagram as separate as possible; with Instagram being nice pictures and this blog discussing processes and items in more depth.

This website is still very much under development, however, all the crucial elements should be in place to keep the site functioning properly.

I hope you find this blog of some interest and if you choose to follow this journey, you have my thanks!
